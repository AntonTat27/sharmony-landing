  pledgee). Identification of a person's identity and status. Access key generation.
how_it_works_item_1_2_1: B. Entering into smart contracts with the platform
how_it_works_item_1_2_2: Transaction Terms and Conditions, Platform Commissions, Settlement and Transfer Mechanisms,
  Security for Performance of Obligations.
how_it_works_item_1_3_1: C. Selecting a transaction target
how_it_works_item_1_3_2: "Two formats are available: Acquiring a property for personal use or Investing in real estate
                           for rent and capitalizing the value of the property."
how_it_works_item_1_4_1: D. Selecting the type of rights
how_it_works_item_1_4_2: "Three formats are available: Ownership of the object (holdsharing), Share in common
                            ownership (rightsharing), Right to use for a fixed period of time (timesharing)."
how_it_works_item_1_5_1: E. Placing a seller's or buyer's offer
how_it_works_item_1_5_2: Placement of data and documents on the object, terms of the transaction, and necessary legal
  procedures.


how_it_works_item_2_1: 2
how_it_works_item_2_2: P2P transaction stage
how_it_works_item_2_1_1: A. Agreeing transaction terms
how_it_works_item_2_1_2: "Characteristics of the object: Price, term, settlement procedure, property transfer,
                           registration obligations, and security of fulfillment."
how_it_works_item_2_2_1: B. Entering into P2P contract
how_it_works_item_2_2_2: Confirmation of agreement, Tokenization of obligations (NFT debt receipts), Signing of the
  P2P Contract.
how_it_works_item_2_3_1: C. Performance Bond
how_it_works_item_2_3_2: "To ensure fulfillment of obligations, the buyer’s and seller’s funds are blocked on accounts
                            until obligations are met."
how_it_works_item_2_4_1: D. Fixing the transaction in blockchain
how_it_works_item_2_4_2: "All transaction data and statuses are fixed in
                            Ethereum / Polygon."
how_it_works_item_2_5_1: E. Opening of escrow accounts
how_it_works_item_2_5_2: Parties open special accounts in a partner bank through the Platform. Escrow agent appointed
  to control fulfillment. Contracts with bank concluded.
  procedures.

how_it_works_item_3_1: 3
how_it_works_item_3_2: Execution of transaction
how_it_works_item_3_1_1: A. Execution of transaction terms and conditions
how_it_works_item_3_1_2: Buyer makes payment and seller transfers object or rights. Acceptance certificate signed.
how_it_works_item_3_2_1: B. Settlement via fiat and digital assets
how_it_works_item_3_2_2: Platform integrated with payment gateways. Escrow funds released and conversions/commissions
  handled.

how_it_works_item_4_2: State registration
how_it_works_item_4_1_1: A. Registration of ownership
how_it_works_item_4_1_2: Ownership/share (holdsharing/rightsharing) via local registry, notary, agent, or law firm.
  Timesharing requires state registration.
how_it_works_item_4_2_1: B. External experts involved in registration
how_it_works_item_4_2_2: SHARMONY can engage realtors, lawyers, and notaries from the appropriate country.

how_it_works_item_5_2: End of deal
how_it_works_item_5_1_1: A. Repayment of liabilities
how_it_works_item_5_1_2: NFT token debt receipts are terminated except for outstanding obligations.
how_it_works_item_5_2_1: B. Updating registers
how_it_works_item_5_2_2: Updating data in the blockchain and state registry (if necessary).
how_it_works_item_5_3_1: C. Finalizing analytics
how_it_works_item_5_3_2: Transaction data automatically analyzed, object assigned new status, information displayed.
