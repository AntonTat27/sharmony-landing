How Sharmony’s P2P platform works

Step-by-step transaction flow with legal protection, smart contracts, and blockchain transparency.

1. Preparing for the transaction

A. Verification of transaction parties (KYC/AML)
Posting data and documents of seller, buyer, beneficiaries and guarantors. Identity verification and access key (digital signature) generation.
B. Entering into smart contracts with the platform
Transaction terms and conditions, platform commissions, settlement mechanisms and security of obligations.
C. Selecting a transaction target
Personal use of property or investment for rental income and capitalization.
D. Selecting the type of rights
Ownership (holdsharing), share in ownership (rightsharing), or right of use for a fixed period (timesharing).
E. Placing a seller’s or buyer’s offer
Placement of documents on the property, transaction terms, and required legal procedures.

2. P2P transaction stage

A. Agreeing transaction terms
Object characteristics, pricing, settlement schedule, transfer procedure and registration obligations.
B. Entering into P2P contract
Tokenization of obligations (NFT debt receipts) and signing of the P2P contract.
C. Performance Bond
Buyer’s and seller’s funds are blocked until obligations are fulfilled.
D. Fixing the transaction in blockchain
All data and statuses are recorded in Ethereum / Polygon.
