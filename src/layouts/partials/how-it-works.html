How Sharmony’s P2P platform works

Step-by-step transaction flow with legal protection, smart contracts, and blockchain transparency.

1

Preparing for the transaction

A. Verification of transaction parties (KYC/AML)
Posting of data and documents on the parties (seller, buyer, beneficiary, guarantor, pledgee). Identification of a person's identity and status. Access key generation.
B. Entering into smart contracts with the platform
Transaction Terms and Conditions, Platform Commissions, Settlement and Transfer Mechanisms, Security for Performance of Obligations.
C. Selecting a transaction target
Two formats are available: Acquiring a property for personal use or Investing in real estate for rent and capitalizing the value of the property.
D. Selecting the type of rights
Three formats are available: Ownership of the object (holdsharing), Share in common ownership (rightsharing), Right to use for a fixed period of time (timesharing).
E. Placing a seller's or buyer's offer
Placement of data and documents on the object, terms of the transaction, and necessary legal procedures.
2

P2P transaction stage

A. Agreeing transaction terms
Characteristics of the object: Price, term, settlement procedure, property transfer, registration obligations, and security of fulfillment.
B. Entering into P2P contract
Confirmation of agreement, Tokenization of obligations (NFT debt receipts), Signing of the P2P Contract.
C. Performance Bond
To ensure fulfillment of obligations, the buyer’s and seller’s funds are blocked on accounts until obligations are met.
D. Fixing the transaction in blockchain
All transaction data and statuses are fixed in Ethereum / Polygon.
E. Opening of escrow accounts
Parties open special accounts in a partner bank through the Platform. Escrow agent appointed to control fulfillment. Contracts with bank concluded.
3

Execution of transaction

A. Execution of transaction terms and conditions
Buyer makes payment and seller transfers object or rights. Acceptance certificate signed.
B. Settlement via fiat and digital assets
Platform integrated with payment gateways. Escrow funds released and conversions/commissions handled.
4

State registration

A. Registration of ownership
Ownership/share (holdsharing/rightsharing) via local registry, notary, agent, or law firm. Timesharing requires state registration.
B. External experts involved in registration
SHARMONY can engage realtors, lawyers, and notaries from the appropriate country.
5

End of deal

A. Repayment of liabilities
NFT token debt receipts are terminated except for outstanding obligations.
B. Updating registers
Updating data in the blockchain and state registry (if necessary).
C. Finalizing analytics
Transaction data automatically analyzed, object assigned new status, information displayed.
